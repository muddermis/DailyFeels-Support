## Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 7, 2026**

### Overview

DailyFeels ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

### Information We Collect

**Mood Data**
- The app stores your mood entries (timestamp and mood value) locally on your device
- If you're signed into iCloud, this data syncs to your personal iCloud account
- We do not have access to your mood data - it remains entirely under your control

**Advertising Data**
- We use Google AdMob to display advertisements
- AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for personalized advertising
- You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

### How We Use Your Information

- **Mood Data**: Used solely to display your mood history and statistics within the app
- **Advertising Data**: Used by Google AdMob to serve relevant advertisements

### Data Storage

- All mood data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework
- If iCloud is enabled, data syncs via Apple's CloudKit to your personal iCloud account
- We do not operate servers that store your personal data

### Third-Party Services

**Google AdMob**
- We use Google AdMob to display advertisements
- AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device's privacy settings

**Apple iCloud**
- If enabled, your data syncs via iCloud
- Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

### Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data - View all your mood entries in the app
- **Export** your data - Use the Export Data feature in Settings
- **Delete** your data - Use the Delete All Data feature in Settings
- **Opt out** of personalized ads - Adjust settings on your device

### Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Open DailyFeels
2. Go to Settings
3. Tap "Delete All Data"
4. Confirm deletion

This will permanently remove all mood entries from your device and iCloud.

### Children's Privacy

DailyFeels is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

### Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- GitHub Issues: https://github.com/muddermis/DailyFeels-Support/issues

## Support

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I track my mood?**
A: Simply tap one of the three mood buttons on the Today screen - the sunny button for good, partly cloudy for okay, or rainy for not great.

**Q: Where is my data stored?**
A: Your data is stored locally on your device. If you're signed into iCloud, it also syncs to your personal iCloud account.

**Q: How do I remove ads?**
A: Go to Settings and tap the "Remove Ads" button to make a one-time purchase of $0.99.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase?**
A: Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your ad-free purchase on a new device.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Go to Settings > Delete All Data. This will permanently remove all your mood entries.

**Q: How do I export my data?**
A: Go to Settings > Export Data to download a CSV file of your mood history.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**
A: Yes! DailyFeels works fully offline. Your data will sync to iCloud when you're back online.
